We are a husband and wife coaching team in the sport of figure skating. I, Laurent Masse and my wife Heidi have been coaching in the Traverse City area for over 20 years. We have had the pleasure to work with so many people in our community and from across the country. I grew up in the Boston, MA. area, where I trained with many great coaches and skaters. Heidi grew up in California where she too had the opportunity to train with high level athletes and coaches. We enjoy coaching all skaters, no matter what their goals are. The skills in skating can be beneficial for a lifetime, and our aim is to be able to share that with our skaters. You can learn more about our bios on the coaches page.
I moved to Traverse City, MI in 1999 just after graduating from college. I was not a skating coach at the time, but moved here because I had attended my best friends wedding here and fell in love with the area. I later began coaching as a temporary way to earn an income. 20+ years later, I am still at it.
Heidi moved here from Massachusettes where she had begun coaching in 2001. She too had visited the area and decided she was going to try to stay in the area for some time and start coaching in Traverse City.

Teaching, Choreoraphing, Sharpening, Music Editing, Equipment and More!

Mission:

To provide skaters with quality instruction, various opportunites and experiences, and to provide what is neccessary for skaters to reach their personal goals.

Objective:

Our objective is to teach the skaters the skills they need to reach their goals in a positive atmosphere. We want the skaters to fully realize the fun this sport can be. TCSkate coaches aim to develop the artist and the athlete.
